Dr. Abdul Qadeer Khan

1936–2021

Father of Pakistan's Nuclear Program

Dr. Abdul Qadeer Khan was a renowned Pakistani nuclear scientist and metallurgical engineer. He played a key role in developing Pakistan's nuclear program and is widely known as the Father of Pakistan's Nuclear Program. His contributions strengthened Pakistan's defense and inspired future generations of scientists. He received several national awards, including Nishan-i-Imtiaz and Hilal-i-Imtiaz, for his outstanding services to the country. Dr. Abdul Qadeer Khan passed away on 10 October 2021, but his legacy continues to be remembered across Pakistan.

Achievements

  • Father of Pakistan's Nuclear Program
  • Established Kahuta Research Laboratories (KRL)
  • Strengthened Pakistan's defense
  • Received Nishan-i-Imtiaz and Hilal-i-Imtiaz
